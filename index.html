Salsa Verde

3
Our Salsa Verde is a bright and tangy blend of fresh tomatillos, zesty lime, and a perfect mix of green chilies. Its refreshing kick and herbaceous notes make it incredibly versatile - perfect for dipping, drizzling over tacos, or enhancing your favorite stews. Freshness you can taste in every spoonful.

Pairs well with :

  • Pulled pork tacos
  • Grilled chicken / beef
  • Quesadillas

Salsa Roja

4
A timeless favorite, our Salsa Roja is crafted with ripe red tomatoes, select dried chilies, and savory spices for a deep, robust flavor with a satisfying heat. This is the authentic red salsa you know and love, perfect for adding a foundational layer of classic Mexican taste to tacos, enchiladas, or simply enjoying with chips. A true taste of tradition in every bite.

Pairs well with :

  • Fish tacos
  • Grilled shrimp
  • Pork chops

Fuego Mango

5
A thrilling dance of sweet and heat! We combine the tropical sweetness of ripe mango with the fiery punch of Manzano and scotch bonnet peppers. This vibrant salsa offers a uniquely balanced flavor experience that's both incredibly addictive and versatile, whether you're pairing it with grilled chicken or using it to give your snacks a sweet and spicy twist.

Who we are

Meet Judith The Salsologist! What started as a family tradition by always having salsa at home and the one bringing salsa to reunions, she decided to expand her audience. Our passion is crafting traditional Mexican salsas using fresh, high-quality ingredients and time-honored methods.
We are proud to be a family-operated business delivering quality, authentic salsas to the community.
